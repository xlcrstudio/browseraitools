Why Use Private AI Tools?

Unlike cloud-based AI tools, every tool on this page runs entirely inside your browser using WebLLM technology. That means there are no privacy risks, no data collection agreements, and no compliance concerns. Because there are no cloud servers involved, every tool is also completely free with no usage limits. Open any tool, start using it instantly, and keep your work private by default.
